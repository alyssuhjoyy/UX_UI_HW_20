Appetit

Making cooking fun with the ingredients you already have!



The Problem


Even though people buy groceries and may have fridge full of food, food delivery apps make it so easy to have food prepared in just a push of a button.

Or going out to eat with friends just seems more fun than cooking a meal at home.

As a result, people end up throwing away food that they didn’t cook before expiration and end up wasting their money.



User Interviews




We found that people generally buy the same items at the grocery store, just the essentials.

Over time, they start to feel unexcited about cooking their food due to repetition and lack of variety.

This causes them to feel like cooking is a chore.
